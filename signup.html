3. Service Description


TexTrack, LLC provides a passive vehicle monitoring service including:


  • Real-time GPS location tracking while the Vehicle is in motion

  • Sixty (60) day rolling location history via PIN-protected control

panel


  • Silent one-way audio monitoring (Listen Mode) — streamed to

Customer's control panel only; the Device emits no sound during

Listen Mode


  • Remote Device screen control

  • Directional routing from Customer's location to Vehicle's location

  • Vehicle power state and battery monitoring

THE SERVICE EXPRESSLY DOES NOT INCLUDE: theft recovery guarantees; law

enforcement notification or coordination; real-time automated alerts or

geofencing; two-way audio; vehicle immobilization; insurance

coordination; or any feature not listed above. TexTrack, LLC is a

passive monitoring tool provider only.
